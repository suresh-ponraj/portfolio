Case Study Details

Unikoin Gold Cryptocurrency Application

Goal

The goal of this application is to provide better user experience for using cryptocurrency and to increase customer satisfaction through that.


Challenges

What does a wallet application have?

How do people use the application?

What can be improved to provide better experience?


The above are the main questions for which I was trying to find a solution. After trying out various wallet application in the market, I made a list of important features which are a must have in a wallet application and those are listed below


  • Dashboard / Home / Overview
  • Coin Price Trends
  • Trading Options
  • Account Settings

Persona

As a starting point, I created a proto-persona based on my own experience I had at cryptocurrency exchange company and by conducting a brief online interview with my friends who have invested in cryptocurrency before.


Guerrilla Usability Testing

Over two days I’ve tested 7 strangers at blockchain related events and cafe in San Francisco. Based on my proto-persona, I checked if they qualify as test users. Following is a simple user demographic of our test users.

After validating test users, based on job story I conducted a guerrilla usability testing. Before the tests, I asked all the participants to imagine the following scenario.


Scenario and Tasks

A friend you trust recommended you to buy UKG. Hearing the fact, you are considering buying UKG

How do you check the price of UKG?

How do you add new cryptocurrency to the home screen?

Let say you decided to buy UKG. If you want to add money to your account how do you do that?

Now you have a balance in your account, how do you buy $5 worth of UKG?


Task Flows

I created a task flow to identify at which points users are struggling to buy cryptocurrency.

Lo-Fi

Based on my research, I sketched out Lo-fi mockups to explored the best possible solutions that will solve identified pain points.

Hi-Fi, Prototyping and Validation

After exploring Lo-fi mockups I used Sketch to create Hi-fi mockups and working prototype to test my solution with users. Following are the changes I made to the app.

All users had difficulties checking the price of coins or buying them. I rearranged the home screen, removed a screen and changed wordings for some buttons so users can complete their tasks with minimal steps intuitively.

In the wallet, most users were confused by the arrangement of navigation and buttons on wallet screen. I re-arranged it and removed some buttons to keep the overall organization simple.
