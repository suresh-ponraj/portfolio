Case Study Details

Road and Traffic Management (Transmax ITS Platform)

Challenge

Transmax’s road agency customers face the challenge of ‘doing more with less’ to manage their road networks. We work together to help achieve their ITS goals such as road safety and efficiency, economic productivity, and reducing traffic congestion and environmental impact.

Phase 1

The project was kick-started with a design workshop to understand the basic brief and formulate a plan of action. Working in collaboration with TMR( Transport and Main roads) we started the discovery process with some product research based on existing desktop apps in a related transmax(Streams Platform). I put together a rough mood board based on the types of interaction, ui trends and functionality that may be required. For example, onboarding, colour pallets, iconography, user control types, personalisation, etc.


Research and collaboration

The User of Transmax are all Local Governments or Transport and Main road in Australia other than NSW Government.

We put together a range of personas based on the demographic most likely to benefit from a new cloud platform for Disruption Management in TMR, How it saves time, How quickly they can respond to the disruptions caused in the Main roads, etc.



Early wireframes and prototypes

I worked primarily in Figma (collaboration in Miro and Figjam) to build a modest picture of the Disruption management application.

This went through a significant number of iterations with feedback from all stakeholders to ensure we were on the right track. From low fidelity wireframes to high fidelity concept screens.

By the end of phase 1, we had an MVC working demo with sufficient functionality to meet the brief so the next step was usability testing.

Insightful Feedback for Phase 2

Reducing wordiness – making sure the app sounds less technical

Changing the language to more informal / friendly

Ways to improve credibility:

  • Make it a list view. Maybe on the map with ability to Show / Hide.
  • Type of source and ability to filter - as some sources are trusted and some not so much.
  • Clean up information in the panel.
  • Users would like to know when an incident was reviewed and responded / dismissed.
  • Users would like to know if an incident is already being looked at.
