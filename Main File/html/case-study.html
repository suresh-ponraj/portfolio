Case Studies

Funhaler

Asthma affects 1 in 10 children in the World. There are many challenges for children, their parents, and their teachers, including proper medication administration, attack prevention, and social exclusion.

We set out to design a solution for our client, Philips, that can support children (4–10 years old) in the transition from parental to self-management. We created FunHaler, a prescription service that provides customized inhalers for children with asthma.

Unikoin Gold Cryptocurrency

Blockchain and cryptocurrency is the future and it’s up to us to use it and make this industry adopt a more human-centric UX, UI, and brand storytelling to achieve mass adoption.

Crypto exchanges are notorious for having poor usability and interfaces overwrought with complexity.

In this application I have used cognitive UX and Human centric design, which helps to focus on the human side of customer service to make it psychologically savvy, economically sound, and easier to scale.
